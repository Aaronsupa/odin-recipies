SPAGHETTI

Easy spaghetti recipe for the whole family!

A classic spaghetti and meatsauce recipie that never gets old.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Ground Beef
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Start by cooking the ground beef
  2. Boil water and cook pasta
  3. Once pasta is finished add a bit of butter into it
  4. Boom! Now you have your ground beef and spaghetti combine them as you see fit!
