SMOOTHIE

Quick strawberry and bannana smoothie for a fast breakfast treat!

A refreshing smoothie with simple ingridents that is great on the go!

Ingredients

  • Strwaberries
  • Bannanas
  • Orange Juice

Steps

  1. Cut strawberries and bannanas and place into blender
  2. Pour orange juice into the blender as well
  3. Blend your Ingredients together
  4. There you go!
